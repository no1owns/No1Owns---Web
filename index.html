About Us

Welcome to Filmmakers Therapy Couch! We're a community of filmmakers, creatives, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders who are dedicated to supporting each other's mental and emotional well-being.

We offer a variety of services, including podcast production in our safe space studio, networking events, and training in DEIB storytelling and impact producing strategies.

Browse our site to learn more about what we have to offer and join our community today!

Presentations

We use film as a form of therapy to heal and spark conversation that catalyzes action.

We create strategy that utilizes screenings, workshops, panel discussions and speaking forums to amplify a message. We use storytelling as a pipeline to evoke inner transformation and therapeutically shift the way we feel about culture and each other.

Events

Why You Should Attend:

  • High-Level Networking: Engage with established and emerging filmmakers, artists, and storytellers, expanding your professional network and collaborative opportunities.
  • Creative Empowerment: Dive into activations designed to ignite your creativity, challenge your thinking, and elevate your projects.
  • A Safe Space for Expression: The Filmmaker's Therapy Couch sessions offer a supportive environment to share experiences, discuss challenges, and uncover solutions through empathy and understanding.
  • Compassion as a Pathway: Explore how compassion not only fosters forgiveness but also serves as a foundational element in storytelling, enriching narratives and deepening connections with audiences.
  • Commune with Creatives: Surrounded by like-minded individuals, you'll find yourself in a safe space where ideas flow freely, and connections are made effortlessly.
  • Empowerment Through Film: Discover how the art of filmmaking can serve as a powerful medium for expressing complex emotions, fostering understanding, and advocating for mental wellness.
  • Mental Wellness Advocacy: This event is a call to action for anyone passionate about mental wellness, looking to make a difference through the creative arts. Whether you're a seasoned filmmaker or an enthusiast, your voice matters.

Special Features:

  • Screening of Thought-Provoking Films: Enjoy a curated selection of films that explore themes of mental health, resilience, and the human condition.
  • Filmmaker's Therapy Couch Sessions: Participate in guided discussions in a supportive environment, where filmmakers and creatives share experiences, challenges, and insights.
  • Networking with Purpose: Connect with individuals who are not just talented and creative but compassionate and empathetic, driven by a desire to impact the world positively.

A Call to Action:

If you're moved by the prospect of enhancing mental wellness through creativity, if you believe in the power of community and the importance of empathy, this event is for you. Come, be part of a gathering where every conversation, every film, and every moment is a step toward understanding, healing, and empowerment.

Connect with Us: If you're a filmmaker, artist, or creative looking to contribute or learn more, we'd love to hear from you. Call us to explore how we can make a difference together.

PSA: If the mission of fostering mental wellness through creativity resonates with you, and you're eager to be part of a compassionate community, join us. Let's make this Leap Year a leap forward for mental health awareness and advocacy.
